Our Story

Hands that
remember.

We started in a two-car garage with a belt sander and a stubborn belief that good bones deserve better skin. Three generations of our family have worked wood — grandpa built barns, dad built cabinets, and we build second chances for furniture the world gave up on.

Every piece that passes through our shop gets stripped, studied, and brought back with intention. We don't flip furniture. We listen to it. The grain tells us what finish it wants. The joints tell us where it hurts. We just do the work.

Based out of the Carolina piedmont.
Shipping anywhere that'll have us.
